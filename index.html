Project Overview

Godrej Crown Residences

By Godrej Properties | Golf Course Township, Greater Noida

Godrej Properties presents Godrej Crown Residences, an ultra-luxury residential development set within a sprawling 100-Acre Golf Township in Greater Noida. Surrounded by a beautifully planned 9-hole golf course, wide roads, lush green landscapes, and world-class infrastructure, the project offers a rare blend of modern comfort and golf-facing living.

Spread across 3.5 Acres with only 3 Exclusive Towers, Godrej Crown Residences ensures low-density living and maximum privacy. Strategically connected via the Yamuna Expressway and Noida–Greater Noida Expressway, and located just 5 mins from Pari Chowk and close to the upcoming Noida International Airport (Jewar).
